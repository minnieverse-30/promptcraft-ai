💡 Prompt Engineering Tips

Write Better Prompts Like a Professional

Small improvements in your prompt can dramatically improve AI responses.

🎯

Be Specific

Clearly describe exactly what you want the AI to do. Detailed prompts produce better results.

🧠

Assign a Role

Tell the AI who it should act as. Example: "You are a Senior Software Engineer."

📋

Specify the Output

Mention whether you want a table, bullet list, code, report, JSON, or explanation.

⚡

Add Constraints

Include tone, audience, word limit, programming language, or formatting requirements.

💬

Provide Context

Give background information so the AI better understands your task.

🔄

Iterate & Improve

Refine your prompt based on the AI's response until you get the desired output.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Have questions? Here are answers to the most common ones.

What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt engineering is the process of designing clear, structured instructions that help AI models produce accurate, useful, and high-quality responses.

Which AI models can I use these prompts with?

PromptCraft AI works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, IBM Granite, GitHub Copilot, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and most modern AI assistants.

Is PromptCraft AI free?

Yes. This project is completely free and open-source. You can customize, improve, and deploy it yourself.

Is my data stored?

No. Your prompts are processed directly in your browser. Nothing is stored on any external server.

Can I copy or download generated prompts?

Yes. You can copy prompts with one click and download them as TXT or Markdown files.

Will more features be added?

Yes! Future updates will include AI API integration, prompt history, favorites, exports, and more.
